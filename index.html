06 · Challenges & Solutions

THREE PROBLEMS.
THREE SOLUTIONS. ZERO LASTING DAMAGE.

01
Rating Dropped to 3.7 Stars

The Problem

After earning Amazon Choice and 100+ units in week one, rating fell to 3.7 on March 21st. All negative reviews: product arriving liquid. Beef tallow naturally liquefies above 70–80°F — Amazon trucks exceed 90°F. Natural characteristic, not a defect. But the listing had no explanation.

The Solution

Added infographic explaining the liquefaction is normal. Client arranged 5-star reviews through his network to urgently restore rating above 4.0.

✓ Rating recovered in days. April revenue: $32,871 — full recovery confirmed. Now at 4.3★ / 80 reviews.
02
Zero History in Competitive Category

The Problem

Launching into cooking fats with no sales history, no reviews, no organic rank. Established competitors had thousands of reviews and strong algorithmic authority. Amazon had no data to evaluate this listing.

The Solution

Dual-track architecture: high-volume keywords for sales velocity + aggressive bids on low/mid volume keywords for early page 1 rankings. Low-volume rankings built authority; high-volume campaigns built reviews and session history.

✓ Amazon Choice in Week 1. Top 20 on a 34,833-search keyword within 30 days. 10 keywords in top 20 within 60 days.
03
Managing ACOS During Aggressive Spend

The Problem

Launch-phase PPC requires aggressive spending to build rank — but overspending without conversion data wastes budget. The tension: spend enough to win impressions and rank, without bleeding budget on non-converting terms.

The Solution

Daily search term monitoring with a strict 7–8 day optimization cycle. Non-converting spend cut quickly. TOS placement reserved exclusively for exact match campaigns with confirmed intent.

✓ Blended ACOS held at 25.1% (Mar) and 27.1% (Apr). Auto Close Match hit 14.3% ACOS and 7.0 ROAS by April.
